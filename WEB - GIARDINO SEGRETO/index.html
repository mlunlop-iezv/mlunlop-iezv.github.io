Our story

A culinary voyage spanning three generations. From the mist of Milan to the wild heart of Andalusia, this is a tale of two lands united by a single obsession: flavor.

"We didn't just bring recipes...

we brought a soul."

- M. BELLINI-SFORZA

For generations, the Bellini family guarded the secrets of northern Italian cuisine. Yet, a restless desire for new horizons took hold. We traveled across Europe searching not just for land, but for a specific terroir. We sought a place that possessed the same wild spirit as the Apennines, a sanctuary where nature still dictated the rhythm of life and where tradition was respected above all else.
When we arrived in the Sierra de Cazorla, the silence spoke to us. The air was heavy with the scent of wild rosemary and pine. We tasted the spicy "Royal" olive oil and knew our search was over. We realized that the intense game meat of these mountains demanded the refinement of our pasta. We did not bring Italy to conquer this land; we brought it to create a new home.
